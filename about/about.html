From Titan, With Love About The Mad Scientist

No one really knows when SKS was born. It would be unfair to pair his birth to the arrival of Eisen, as he's a bit newer to the incarnate soul formerly known as Neuton, but nonetheless SKS has been around for what seems like eons incarnating his monadic intellect and wisdom through whom he deems worthy ancestral descendants.

What separates SKS from most composers is his outright passion to never settle for less. Since his introduction he's been a relentess force of inspiration to the newly evolved YL Eisen as well as a guiding light and instructor to producers and artists alike!
